Lasagna

Description

Making delicious lasagna at home is easy!

First, you will need to gather your ingredients:

Ingredients

  • 12 whole wheat lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 tsp dried oregano, or to taste
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 1/2 cups tomata-basil pasta sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles
    and cook for 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. Meanwhile, place ground beef, garlic, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and
    black pepper in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir until beef
    is crumbly and evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
  4. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, and eggs together in a large bowl
    until thoroughly combined.
  5. Lay 4 noodles side by side on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking pan; top
    with a layer of prepared tomato-basil sauce, a layer of ground beef mixture,
    and a layer of cottage cheese mixture. Repeat layers twice more, ending with
    a layer of sauce; sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top. Cover with foil.
  6. Bake in preheated oven until lasagna is bubbling and cheese has melted,
    about 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake until cheese has begun to brown,
    about 10 more minutes. Allow to stand at least 10 minutes before serving.
